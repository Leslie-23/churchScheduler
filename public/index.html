How It Works

From signup to a fully automated schedule in under 5 minutes.

01

Sign Up & Create Your Unit

Create a free account with email or Google. Set up your unit — "Ushering Team", "Media Unit", whatever fits. You'll get an invite code to share with your team.

02

Add Your Members

Enter each member's name, phone, gender, and suit status. Rate their skills (1-5 stars) for each position. This tells the system who's qualified for what.

03

Configure Positions

In Settings, set how many people you need per position for each service type. Example: 4 door stewards for Sunday, 2 for midweek.

04

Generate a Schedule

Create a service, pick the date and type, hit Generate. The system assigns every position based on skill, fairness, and availability.

05

Publish & Notify

Review the roster, swap anyone if needed, then publish. Send SMS to everyone — each person gets their name, position, date, and time.

06

Track & Improve

After the service, mark attendance. No-shows get flagged. Over time, reliability scores adjust and scheduling gets smarter.
