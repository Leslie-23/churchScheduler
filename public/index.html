How It Works

From signup to a fully automated schedule in under 5 minutes. Here's exactly what to do — step by step.

01

Sign Up & Create Your Unit

Create a free account with your email or Google. Then create a new unit — give it a name like "Ushering Team" or "Media Unit". You'll get an invite code to share with your team.

02

Add Your Team Members

Add each member with their name, phone number, gender, and whether they have a suit. Then rate their skills (1-5 stars) for each position. This tells the system who's good at what.

03

Set Your Positions & Counts

In Settings, choose how many people you need for each position per service type. For example: 4 door stewards and 2 ushers for Sunday, 2 door stewards for Wednesday.

04

Generate a Schedule

Go to Schedule, create a new service (pick a date, type, and slot), then hit "Generate". The system assigns everyone automatically based on their skills, rotation history, and availability.

05

Publish & Notify

Review the schedule, make any swaps if needed, then publish it. Send SMS notifications to everyone with one click — each person gets their name, position, and date.

06

Track & Improve

After the service, mark attendance. Members who don't show up get flagged for follow-up. Over time, the system learns who's reliable and schedules accordingly.
