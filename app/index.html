How to Use Stewardly

Getting Started

  1. Create or join a unit — After signing up, create a new service unit (e.g. "Ushering Team") or join one using an invite code from your leader.
  2. Add your team members — Go to the Members page and add each person with their name, phone, gender, and whether they own a suit. Set their skill ratings for each position.
  3. Set up position counts — In Settings, configure how many people you need for each position per service type (e.g. 4 door stewards for Sunday).
  4. Create a service and generate — Go to Schedule, create a new service (pick a date and type), then hit Generate. The system assigns everyone automatically.

Understanding the Schedule

When you generate a schedule, the system considers 4 factors to pick the best person for each position:

  • Skill Rating — How well they can handle that position (1-5 stars)
  • Fair Rotation — People who haven't served recently get priority
  • Variety — People aren't stuck at the same position every time
  • Reliability — Members with good attendance records score higher

The system also respects position requirements — if a position requires a suit or is male-only, only eligible members are considered.

1st & 2nd Service

If your church runs two services, you can schedule members for each one separately:

  • When adding a member, set their Service Availability — "Both Services", "1st Only", or "2nd Only"
  • When creating a service, choose the Service Slot — 1st or 2nd
  • The scheduler will only assign members who are available for that slot

Replacing Someone

If a member can't make it:

  1. Open the service schedule
  2. Click "Can't Make It" next to their name
  3. The system suggests ranked replacements with scores and reasons
  4. Pick one and confirm — the original member gets marked unavailable for that date

Attendance Tracking

After a service, mark who showed up:

  1. Open the service schedule
  2. Click "Mark Attendance"
  3. Mark each person as Present, Absent, or Excused
  4. Click "Save Attendance"

Members with poor attendance (below 70%) appear in the Needs Follow-Up card on the dashboard. Their reliability score also drops, meaning the scheduler will prefer more reliable members.

SMS Notifications

Send duty reminders or broadcast messages to your team via SMS:

  • Duty Notifications — Select a service and send personalized messages to every assigned member (their name, position, and date)
  • Broadcast — Send a custom message to all active members (for meetings, announcements, etc.)

Members without phone numbers are automatically skipped.

Custom Positions & Service Types

The default positions (Door, Standing, Usher, etc.) can be customized by the unit owner:

  • Go to Settings
  • Add, edit, or remove Service Types (e.g. add "Friday Service")
  • Add, edit, or remove Position Types (e.g. add "Sound Desk" or "Parking")
  • Set requirements: whether a position needs a suit or is male-only
  • After changing positions/types, update your Position Counts to set how many people each slot needs

Roles

  • Owner — Full control: manage members, schedules, settings, invite codes, roles, custom positions/types
  • Admin — Can manage members, create schedules, send SMS, view reports
  • Member — Can view schedules and their own profile

Troubleshooting

"Schedule generated with warnings"

This means some positions couldn't be fully filled. You either don't have enough members, or available members don't meet the position requirements (e.g. suit, gender). Add more members or adjust position counts.

"A service already exists on this date"

You already created a service of this type for this date and slot. Check the schedule page — it's already there.

SMS not sending

Check that the ARKESEL_API_KEY is set in your server environment. Members without phone numbers are skipped. Phone numbers must be valid Ghana numbers.

"Not a member of this unit"

You may have been removed from the unit, or your session is pointing to the wrong unit. Try switching units in the top bar or logging out and back in.

Page shows blank or spinner stuck

Refresh the page. If it persists, check your internet connection. If you're an admin, make sure the server is running.
